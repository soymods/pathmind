### Visual Automation
- **Node-Based Editor**: Build automation visually with nodes, connections, and editable parameters instead of commands or scripts
- **Readable Workflows**: Lay out your logic in a way that is easy to follow, revisit, and expand over time
- **Creative Sandbox**: Experiment with behaviors, interactions, and systems inside a dedicated in-game workspace

### Workspace And Presets
- **Saved Graphs**: Keep your automation setups as reusable presets you can come back to later
- **Organized Editing**: Work across multiple presets and continue refining them as your ideas grow
- **Import And Share**: Bring in community-made setups or use your own presets as building blocks for larger systems

### In-Game Execution
- **Live Workflow Runtime**: Run graphs directly in Minecraft and watch them respond while you play
- **Visual Feedback**: See execution state, runtime feedback, and validation cues while building and testing
- **Flexible Logic**: Combine actions, conditions, reusable logic, and event-driven behavior into larger workflows

### Navigation And Interaction
- **Movement Automation**: Build workflows around movement, navigation, and world interaction
- **Expanded Integrations**: Optional integrations can extend what Pathmind can do in supported setups
- **Built For Play**: Designed to feel like a Minecraft tool, not an external scripting environment

### Community Marketplace
- **Browse Presets In-Game**: Discover automation setups made by other players without leaving Minecraft
- **Publish Your Own Work**: Share your presets with the community through the built-in marketplace
- **Start From Examples**: Import existing presets and adapt them to your own world, style, or ideas

## Quick Start

### Prerequisites
- **Minecraft**: 1.21 through 1.21.11
- **Fabric Loader**: 0.17.3 or higher
- **Fabric API**: version matching your Minecraft version
- **Java**: 21 or higher

### Installation

1. **Install Fabric Loader**
   - Download and install Fabric Loader for your Minecraft version
   - [Download from FabricMC](https://fabricmc.net/use/installer/)
2. **Install Fabric API**
   - Download the matching Fabric API release for your Minecraft version
   - [Download from Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api)
3. **Install Pathmind**
   - Download the Pathmind jar for your Minecraft version
   - Place it in your `mods` folder
4. **Launch and Enjoy**
   - Start Minecraft with Fabric
   - Use your configured keybind to open the visual editor

## How to Use

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/6hy3GnehBpA" allowfullscreen></iframe>

### Opening the Editor
- Use your configured keybind to open Pathmind
- The visual editor opens in fullscreen mode

### Creating Workflows
1. **Add Nodes**: Drag nodes from the sidebar onto the workspace
2. **Connect Logic**: Link nodes together to shape how the workflow behaves
3. **Configure Parameters**: Edit node values to match the behavior you want
4. **Organize Your Graph**: Move nodes around to keep your setup readable
5. **Run And Refine**: Test your workflow in-game and keep improving it
